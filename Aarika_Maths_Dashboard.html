Worked Examples

Example 1: Convert 5 hours 15 minutes to minutes

📌 We know: 1 hour = 60 minutes
▶ 5 hours = 5 × 60 = 300 minutes
▶ Add the extra 15 minutes: 300 + 15
✅ Answer = 315 minutes

Example 2: Convert 1 year to hours

📌 1 year = 365 days, 1 day = 24 hours
▶ 365 × 24 = 8,760
✅ Answer = 8,760 hours

Comparing Fractions

When the bottom number (denominator) is the same, the fraction with the bigger top number is bigger!

4/10 > 3/10 because 4 > 3
3/11 < 10/11 because 3 < 10
8/10 < 9/10 because 8 < 9
⬆️ Ascending Order (small to big):
1/10 < 3/10 < 4/10 < 7/10 < 9/10
⬇️ Descending Order (big to small):
9/10 > 7/10 > 4/10 > 3/10 > 1/10
➕➖

Add & Subtract Fractions

💡 Rule: When bottom numbers are the same — just add/subtract the top numbers. Bottom stays the same!

3/7 + 4/7 = ?
3 + 4 = 7, same bottom
7/7 = 1 (whole!)
= 1
3/12 + 4/12 + 4/12 = ?
3 + 4 + 4 = 11, bottom stays 12
= 11/12
2/9 + 4/9 = ?
2 + 4 = 6, bottom stays 9
= 6/9
5/10 − 2/10 = ?
5 − 2 = 3, bottom stays 10
= 3/10
12/15 − 9/15 = ?
12 − 9 = 3, bottom stays 15
= 3/15
9/12 − 8/12 = ?
9 − 8 = 1, bottom stays 12
= 1/12
